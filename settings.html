ZXQM Copyright

Artist profiles

ZXQM isn't a viable option to support artists.

Because of that, ZXQM has added all the artist accounts in the profile.

So, you can follow, listen and support the artist in other platform.

Copyrighted songs

All the ZXQM songs are uploaded to a data server.

All the users can add songs, for safety measures the songs are checked for a mod before uploading.

After saying that, the artist has all the privileges to delete a song from our site.

If any artist want to delete his songs from the data server, just send a mail to support@zxqm.cf

ZXQM terms

ZXQM is in a growing stage, please make sure you have shared the page.

If you have any suggest to zxqm you can send it to suggest@zxqm.cf

If you have any problem, just report it to support@zxqm.cf
